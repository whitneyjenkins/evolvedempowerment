About Us

Evolved Empowerment Project Inc. is a nonprofit organization rooted in Phoenix, Arizona, co-founded by Whitney Jenkins and Stacey Vernon. We are committed to elevating women and youth who are overcoming trauma, hardship, or life transitions through beauty-based healing, mentorship, and personal development. Our leadership and programs reflect a deep understanding of what it means to rebuild from the inside out.

Guided by the belief that transformation begins with self-worth, our organization centers compassion, community, and creative empowerment. From workshops and support groups to personal styling sessions and educational tracks, we meet each individual where they are—and help them evolve forward with confidence and purpose.
